《花心小丑》_北京国际青年戏剧节_国际在线
  

海报

编剧：李宝群

导演\演员：查文浩

故事梗概：

一个雪花飞舞、寒风凛冽的冬季；

A chilly winter with heavy snow;

一座钢筋水泥、车流不息、人流不息的城市森林；

A city full of armored concretes, cars and people;

一个骑着车子给人送花的“小丑”…….

A clown who sends flowers to people with his bike;

他，很卑微，却很快乐；很孤独，却很幸福；

He is humble, but happy; he is lonely, but blessed;

他，很渺小，却很善良；很忧伤，却很坚强；

He is tiny, but kind; he is sad, but strong;

他心中有很多花儿在开放，他前边有很多梦儿在飞翔…...

Many flowers blossom in his heart, and many dreams are flying around him.

他的故事从早晨开始，到夜晚还没有结束……

His story begins from the early morning and till night is not over.

这一天很长，很长…...

It’s a long, long day…

导演介绍：

查文浩

毕业于中央戏剧学院2008级导演系本科班，主要戏剧作品有 话剧《秦王政》， 话剧《别折腾》， 话剧《自选题》， 自导自演小品《爷爷变成了幽灵》获得
2010年中央戏剧学院小品大赛获最佳小品、最佳导演、最受观众欢迎小品奖。

Zha Wenhao, graduated from Central Academy of Drama 2008 Director
undergraduate classes. Major theatre works: drama, ”Qin Wangzheng”, drama, “Do
Not Toss”, drama, ”Zi Xuanti”, and the Pieces, ”Grandpa Became the Ghost”
which direct and act by himself won the Best Comedy, Best Director and the
most Popular Comedy at the Central Academy of Drama Pieces Competition in
2010.

导演的话：

如果有一天小丑哭了，你会不会觉得他在搞笑？我总能看见送花小丑在街上卖力的蹬着自行车，他的脸上被口红涂满笑容，像一阵快乐的彩色旋风飞驰而过。其实面具后的他和我们一样普通平凡，总是一个人在舞台上卖力地独舞，滑稽的演绎着自己的悲伤。我们又何尝不是一样？

Words of the director:

If one day the clown cried, would you think he was funny? I can always see the
clown who sends flowers to the people is riding bike hardly in the street,
with smiles drew by the lipsticks in his face, just like a burst of happy
colorful whirlwind speed past. But in fact, after the mask, the clown is as
ordinary as us, always dancing on the stage alone to acting his own sorrows.
Wouldn’t’t we be the same?
